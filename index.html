Welcome

The National Aeromodelling and Aviators Society (NAAS) promotes all aspects of building and flying model aircraft.

We frequently host flying events at out field located in the Namadgi National Park.

We welcome interested visitors from the public and guest fliers from other clubs. We usually fly on weekends and public holidays.
