National Aeromodelling and Aviators Society

Willie Emmett Airfield, Namadgi National Park, ACT, Australia

Welcome

The National Aeromodelling and Aviators Society (NAAS) promotes all aspects of building and flying model aircraft.

We maintain a flying field located in the Namadgi National Park and frequently host national-wide flying events.

We welcome interested visitors from the public and guest fliers from other clubs. We usually fly the weekends and public holidays.
