--- title: About ---

The National Aeromodelling and Aviators Society, NAAS, was founded in 2013. It is an MAAA affiliated club.

Members Passions

Our members represent a variety of passions in the hobby:

  • Scale models: building (from scratch) and flying, predominantly WW1
  • Aerobatics: IMAC and 3D
  • Gliders and gliding
  • ARFs and recreational ("sport") flying
  • FPV
  • Organizing events
